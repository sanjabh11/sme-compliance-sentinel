# SME Workspace Sentinel: Winning Strategy Audit

## Confidence Position

I cannot be certain this will win the hackathon. A certainty claim would be false because judge preferences, competitor traction, and real revenue outcomes are unknowable. The defensible target is:

- Full current-rule readiness.
- High technical differentiation.
- Measurable customer traction before submission.
- A market narrative that is narrower and sharper than generic compliance automation.

## USP

**One-day Google Workspace risk scan that turns risky sharing and sensitive-data exposure into SOC2 readiness evidence and sales-ready trust proof for seed-stage teams.**

This is narrower than Vanta/Drata/Secureframe and stronger for a hackathon demo because it shows a live operational loop: Workspace event -> hybrid scanner -> Gemini explanation -> human-approved remediation -> evidence room -> revenue/user proof.
